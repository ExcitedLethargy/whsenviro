About Power Usage

Welcome to the Whakatane High School Enviro Club's website for energy audits!

This site was developed to assist the Enviro Club with their energy audits. Every year, huge spreadsheets are used to record each audit, requiring countless hours of work from a number of people. This site intends to rationalise that process by making everything electronic and automatic, so that far less people need to be 'data monkeys' to allow the audits to take place.

To find the other pages in the site, click on the left-hand side of this page to open the menu - click anywhere else to exit it. Alternatively, click here.

For WHS Enviro Club members, sign in here, or alternatively do so with the menu.
